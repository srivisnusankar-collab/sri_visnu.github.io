Projects

Multi-Cavity Injection Mould

Designed and developed a multi-cavity injection mould for polypropylene (PP) components using a stripper plate ejection system. The project involved machine selection, cavity calculation based on shot capacity, runner and gate design, shrinkage allowance consideration, and clamping force estimation using standard mould design data. The mould was optimized for efficient production, proper alignment, and reliable ejection of components.

Multi-Directional Rotating Trailer

Developed a mechanical design model of a trailer capable of unloading materials in multiple directions. Designed the rotating mechanism and support structure considering load distribution, safety and stability. The project aimed to reduce unloading time and improve operational flexibility in construction and agricultural applications.
